Hey.

I'm Landon Tubesing, a designer of sorts from Atlanta.
I'm currently a first year student at Georgia Tech, majoring in computational media.
I've done 2D work in the form of drawing since as far back as elementary school, but have only gotten into digital media in the last 5 years or so.

Hobbies and interests

I'm a huge music nerd, having played a range of musical instruments and delved into the world of music production.
Most of my favorite music is electronic, ranging from noise and drone to dance music.
A few of my favorites are Nicolas Jaar, Arca, and The Microphones.
If you're curious about the music I make, you can see my SoundCloud page here.

Games

My heart has always had a bit of a soft spot for relaxing (or sometimes not at all relaxing) singleplayer games.

My favorite series are Ratchet and Clank, WipeEout, and Hollow Knight.
